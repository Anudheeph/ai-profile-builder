# Satya Nadella
_CEO of Microsoft_

## Executive Summary
Satya Nadella is the current Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Microsoft [3], leading major projects including the company's move to cloud computing [1]. He has been CEO since 2014 [2][3][4]. Nadella is an Indian-born business executive [2] with a commitment to continuous learning [9].

## Biography / Summary
Satya Nadella is the current chairman and CEO of Microsoft, having joined the company in 1992 [4][8] and risen to CEO in 2014 [2][3][4]. He grew up in the southern Indian city of Hyderabad [2] and studied electrical engineering at Mangalore University [2].
